# The GridOne Manifesto

## Don't just use AI. Help build it.

Artificial intelligence may become one of humanity's most transformative tools. Yet the infrastructure that shapes it is available to only a small number of organizations.

Meanwhile, billions of people own devices with useful computing capacity. Most can consume AI, but they have no simple, secure, and rewarding way to participate in the infrastructure behind it.

GridOne exists to explore a different relationship between people and AI.

We believe people should be more than passive users.

We believe idle technology can become collective progress.

We believe participation should be voluntary, understandable, reversible, and under the Builder's control.

We believe privacy and security are product requirements, not marketing claims.

We believe useful contributions should earn useful access—not speculative tokens detached from the network's purpose.

We believe centralized infrastructure and community infrastructure can complement each other.

We believe the value created by a collaborative network should flow back to the people who make it possible.

GridOne has no passive users. It has **Builders**: people who choose to contribute idle capacity, earn AI Credits, and help test a more participatory model for AI.

The future of AI should not only be delivered to humanity.

It should be built **with humanity**.

**One grid. Millions of Builders. A shared future.**
